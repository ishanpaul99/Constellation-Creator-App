About

More than a drawing tool.

Constellation Creator is a creative celestial design tool that allows users to craft their own constellations, generate legends, and build a personal collection of stories written among the stars.

Every creation is a peaceful, mystical sky chart where your imagination becomes a story, and every star feels like a secret waiting to be shared.
